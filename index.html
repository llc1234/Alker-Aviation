Drone Types

Photography Drone

Equipped with ultra-high resolution cameras and stabilization systems to capture cinematic aerial imagery for media, real estate, and marketing.

Industrial Inspection Drone

Specialized drones for infrastructure inspections — including bridges, towers, and pipelines — using both visual and infrared imaging to detect structural issues before they become critical.

Infrared (IR) Inspection Drone

Advanced UAVs with thermal cameras for detecting heat loss, electrical faults, and hidden structural problems. Ideal for solar panel inspections, power line monitoring, and industrial safety checks.
